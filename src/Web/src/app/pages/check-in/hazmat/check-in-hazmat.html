Prohibited and Restricted Items

Certain items are restricted or prohibited from being carried in your baggage. By continuing, you confirm that your baggage does not contain any of the following:

  • Explosives, fireworks, or flares
  • Compressed gases (including aerosols beyond permitted limits)
  • Flammable liquids or solids (including lighter fuel)
  • Oxidising substances and organic peroxides
  • Toxic or infectious substances
  • Radioactive materials
  • Corrosives (including batteries with free liquid)
  • Items that could ignite or cause harm during the flight

Failure to comply may result in prosecution. If you are unsure whether an item is permitted, please check with airport staff before proceeding.
